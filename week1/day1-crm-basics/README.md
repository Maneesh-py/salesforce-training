Day 1 - CRM Basics

1.What is CRM?
CRM (Customer Relationship Management) is a system used by companies to manage customer information, communication, and sales activities.

2.Why Companies Use Salesforce
Companies use salesforce for:
- Managing customers
- Tracking sales
- Storing business data
- Improving productivity
- To Generate reports
## Account
Represents a company or organization.
## Contact
Represents a person in the company.
## Opportunity
Represents a possible business deal.
# Business Flow
Lead → Contact → Opportunity → Customer
A Lead is a person who shows interest for the first time.
After getting proper details, the lead becomes a Contact.
An Opportunity is the possible deal or service being discussed.
When everything is successful, the person becomes a Customer.
# Real-World Mapping (College Admission)
- Account = College
- Contact = Student
- Lead = Student asking for admission
- Opportunity = Admission process
